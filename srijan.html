About Event

SRIJAN is the annual Mega Event of PRAYAAS which happens around the October. This event is an attempt to club up PRAYAAS kids with NITJ students and hence reviving the innocent, playful child inside each one of us. This teaches the kids various qualities like team work, creativity, etc. It hosts various games like Musical Chairs, Slate race, etc along-with a mega Drawing competition.
